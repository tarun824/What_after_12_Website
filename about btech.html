B Tech (Bachelor of Technology) Overview

B Tech is the abridged form of Bachelor of Technology. It is a professional four-year undergraduate course that covers all the fields of engineering as well as technology. BTech is available in various specializations like BTech computer science (CSE), BTech Biotechnology engineering, BTech Information Technology engineering (IT), BTech mechanical engineering (ME), BTech electronics and communication engineering  (ECE),  BTech civil engineering (CE),  BTech in electrical engineering (EE), etc. Apart from the core engineering field, B Tech allows candidates to work in the Information Technology, Data Analysis, Consultancy, and Management sectors. BTech graduates having good technical skills can also apply for jobs in the public sectors and various Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs). B. Tech courses can be pursued in both online and offline modes. 

The BTech course is designed for developing the technical skills of candidates who aim to invest themselves in the core technology and development industry of our economy. It comprises both workings of theoretical and practical knowledge of an individual. Students are offered admission on the basis of state level, national level, and university level entrance exams such as JEE Main, WBJEE, BITSAT, KLEEE, AP EAMCET, and JEE Advanced (applicable only for IITs), etc.

  • Learning BTech will provide candidates with the procedure to find key solutions for the problems trending in the industrial field
  • Engineers are the ones responsible for creating and developing new logarithms that are beneficial for upcoming technologies
  • The demand for engineers or BTech graduates has always been in the boom, as they form the core of the companies while maintaining the upcoming trends in their engineering policies
  • The average salary of an experienced engineer can be as high as Rs. 2- 2.5 Lakhs per annum. The average salary of a fresher engineer can be Rs. 40 to Rs 50 Lakhs per annum

B Tech Course Eligibility Criteria

The eligibility criteria required for admission to B Tech are as follows -

  • Min Qualification Required for the candidates to apply in BTech: Candidates have to pass 10+2 from a recognized board or equivalent
  • Min. Marks Required (Aggregate) for the candidates to apply in BTech: Candidates have to secure 50% marks in aggregate. The maximum marks can be as high as to 70% depending on college to college
  • Age Limit that the candidates should maintain while applying for BTech courses: Some colleges and entrance exams can have a restriction on age for all the aspirants who aspire to take admission in B Tech courses. Candidates should be at least 18 years of age and not be more than 24 years of age counted from the date of the examination

How to Get Admission in B tech?

To get admission in engineering, below are the sequence of steps that have to be followed by the candidates before applying for BTech courses.

  • Candidates in order to be admitted to BTech have to appear for an entrance exam
  • There are various national level entrance exams like JEE Main, which is accepted by NITs, GFTIs, and JEE Advanced which is accepted exclusively by IITs
  • Other than the exams mentioned above, there are other state-level exams that are accepted by many colleges like WBJEE, AP EAMCET, TS EAMCET, KEAM, and MHT CET. other university-based entrance exams such as VITEEE, SRMJEEE, BITSAT, UPESEAT, etc
  • The entrance exams are conducted mostly in April, May, and June every year
  • After the declaration of the results, it is followed by counseling, and the seat allotment process has been conducted by the concerned authorities. During the counseling process, candidates will have to choose the college and courses of their choice based on the rank acquired by them
  • After the college is chosen, candidates will have to visit the campus of the respective college for conducting the admission formalities

B Tech Course Entrance Exams

There are numerous entrance exams in India, divided into national, state level, and university levels. The national level top B Tech entrance exams are JEE Main, JEE Advanced state-level BTech exams include WBJEE, MHT CET; And university level exams include BITSAT, VITEEE, and SRMJEEE. To know more about these exams, read the following.

JEE Main

The Joint Entrance Examination is conducted by the national testing agency or NTA. It is conducted for admissions in NIT, IIITs, GFTIs, and other institutions all over India.

JEE Advanced

The JEE Advanced is conducted for admissions in IITs. Candidates who come under the ranks of 2.5 Lakhs are eligible to apply and sit for JEE Advanced. 

WBJEE

WBJEE is conducted by the West Bengal joint entrance examination board. It is conducted for admissions in undergraduate B Tech courses.  

MHT CET

MHT CET is conducted by the state common entrance test cell of Maharashtra. The exam is conducted on the basis of class 11 and class 12 syllabi.  

BITSAT

BITSAT is conducted by the Birla Institute of Technology & Science, Pilani for students who are interested in taking admissions in the Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad campuses. 

VITEEE

VITEEE is conducted by the Vellore Institute of Technology for candidates who are interested in taking admissions for the Vellore and the Chennai campus.

Scope of B Tech in India

Individuals who have completed their engineering course are added the prefix depending on the specialization that they have opted for. Candidates who have pursued BTech courses will have a choice, either to join in the job or continue with their MTech or some other master’s course. Master’s in Technology or MTech can be pursued after one completes his or her Bachelor of Technology in a particular specialization. While completing their master’s, candidates will be specializing in a particular field only. 

Candidates can also choose to study for master's along with their job. They can apply for distance education. There are numerous distance mode colleges that provide quality education to the students opting for it.  Securing a higher degree will not only enhance your study skills but also highlight your position in the professional field. 

If the candidates after pursuing the BTech course are having leadership skills, then they can proceed with their own start-ups. With the emergence of new technologies and styles, new engineering graduates are starting up their own businesses.

When speaking about Bachelor of Technology jobs, there is a lot of abundance in the facilities as the BTech course is very diverse. One can opt for jobs in private and public sector undertakings too, depending on the criteria met by the company. 

B Tech Salary

Bachelor of Technology salary received by the graduates at the initial level is moderate. For the professional field, candidates need to be experienced to earn a healthy amount of BTech salary every year. Once experience is gained, the earnings are beyond expectations. 

The starting BTech salary of an engineer is around Rs. 20 K to Rs. 30 K per month. After the experience is gained by the graduate, the BTech salary is around Rs. 5 to 10 Lakhs per annum spending on the scope. The salary of an experienced B Tech engineer may be more than Rs. 15 Lakhs per annum. Experienced engineers are also demanded abroad with a healthy package.
