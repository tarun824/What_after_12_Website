KCET 2022 Counselling Procedure

The counselling procedure for KCET will be based on the ranks scored by the candidates in the main examination.

KCET 2022 Counselling Document Verification

  • After registration, the next step in KCET counselling is the document verification process.

  • For the verification process, candidates have to carry original documents with them.

  • Candidates have to bring the required documents in original with one set of attested photocopies and two Passport size photographs for the verification process.

  • An acknowledgement card and a verification slip are issued to the candidates after the verification process ends.

  • After the verification of documents is done, candidates’ eligibility under different criteria is also verified on the same day.

  • A User ID and password are given to the students who successfully clear the verification round.

KCET 2022 Registration

  • After the document verification, candidates will have to register themselves at the counselling centre.

  • Details of the verification slip are to be added carefully in the registration form.

KCET 2022 Counselling Choice Filling and Seat Allocation

  • Seat allotment and choice filling will be done online.

  • Candidates must visit the official website and fill in their choice of college and course.

  • Candidates will have to log in with the user ID and password issued to them after the completion of the verification round.

  • To fill and lock the choice of colleges and courses, candidates’ will have to login by entering the CET number.

  • The allotment of seats will be based on the selection of the courses and colleges done by the candidates.

  • The category-wise, course-wise and college-wise seat matrix will be uploaded on the official website.

  • There will be no limit to the number of options being entered.

  • After the first round of seat allotment, the unfilled/surrendered/cancelled or newly added seats will be offered as Casual Vacancies. The allotment will be the same as the first round.

KCET 2022 Counselling Fee Submission

  • Payment of fee has to be done after the admission process completes.

  • Payment of the fee will confirm the admission of the candidates.

KCET 2022 Counselling Important Notes

  • After the second round of counselling of KCET, if the seats remain vacant, the unfilled/cancelled/newly added seats will be offered as Casual Vacancies.

  • After verification, the original documents will be returned to the candidates and the set of photocopies will be retained by KEA.

  • If any candidate fails to produce any of the documents, the document verification of the candidate will not be done and he/she will not be allowed to participate in the KCET counselling process.

  • Only eligible candidates, after document verification, can enter their choices.

  • During the first round of counselling for KCET 2022, the seat allotment for Special Category seats for Physically Disabled, NCC and Sports Category will be done.

  • After receiving the seat matrix from the Government, the option entry will start for the candidates and the details regarding the category-wise, course-wise and college-wise available seats for allotment will be made available on the official KEA website in downloadable and print format

  • Only those candidates will be eligible for the option entry process whose verification has been done by the concerned authorities. The candidates will be able to choose from the available list of colleges/courses. They can also choose from the available seats on display during the option entry step

  • The candidates are suggested to only go for those options in the discipline in which they are eligible. Candidates eligible for multiple disciplines can exercise their option entry in the same form

  • The form for option entry will be displayed on the screen as soon as the candidates enter their preliminary information at the specified URL on the internet

  • The candidates have to mention the priority number in front of the course or college displayed in which they are interested in and are eligible for

  • There is no limit for option entry that is why the candidates are suggested to enter the maximum possible number of entries to eliminate the possibility of not securing a seat

  • The options entered by the candidates must be in decreasing order of their priority

  • Before filling in their options, the candidates are advised to do thorough research about the course/college they want admission into

  • The candidates can alter their choices made till the last date. Once satisfied with their option entry, the candidates have to click on the “submit” button followed by clicking on the “log out” button

  • The candidates are suggested to take a printout of the option entry page once the process gets over

The Government will issue the seat matrix for each course, college and category to be available on the official website of the Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA). The final seat allotment process will be conducted in three rounds where each round will comprise three phases and each phase consisting of a number of iterations.
