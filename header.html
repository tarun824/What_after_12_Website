Who we are

Autonomous institution with NAAC-A accreditation

Global Academy of Technology is an A-grade college that is counted one 
amongst the best engineering colleges in Bangalore. Along with being equipped 
with modern technology and the perfect infrastructure, the college has an ambience
and culture to accelerate learning.

The Management, Principal and Staff of the institution believe in
the overall development of the students and hence encourage them to participate
in co-curricular, extra-curricular and sports events.
